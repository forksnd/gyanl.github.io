ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Gyan. I like technology, design and cats.

I have a a master's degree in Interaction Design from IDC School of Design, and I spent 4 years designing digital products at Microsoft. These days I'm a member at South Park Commons Bangalore, I teach a class on Ergonomics for Design Students, and I'm running Public Knowledge Studio. I use this website to document my work and some stray thoughts.
